Notes from running a design workshop with university students.

The workshop focused on basic visual design principles: hierarchy, contrast, alignment, repetition, and proximity. We worked through exercises using only black rectangles on a white canvas.

Constraints make better learning. By limiting the tools (just rectangles), students could focus on the principles rather than getting lost in software features.
